Privacy Policy — Termora

Last updated: 21 June 2026

Termora is a local-first SSH client for iPhone. It is designed so your data stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. Termora has no user accounts, no analytics, and no servers operated by the developer. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data stored on your device

  • Host details (hostname, port, username) and SSH key metadata are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData.
  • Passwords and private keys are stored only in the iOS Keychain, protected by device encryption and (optionally) Face ID / passcode.
  • Server host-key fingerprints are stored locally to protect you against man-in-the-middle attacks.

Network

Termora connects directly from your device to the servers you choose. Your connections do not pass through any servers operated by the developer.

Third parties

Termora contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
